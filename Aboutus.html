Company Profile

Allinol is a petrochemical product and flammable liquid used as a fuel for spark-ignited internal combustion engines, invented by billionaire, inventor and explorer Miles Axlerod.

Axlerod attracts media attention with his solo journey around the world without GPS but disappears after running out of fuel in the desert. He resurfaces 36 days later, claiming to have created a new fuel from natural elements.

Upon his return, he announces the sale of his oil company and his conversion to electricity, focusing on researching clean and renewable fuel. He commercializes this ethanol under the name Allinol.
